## What They Show

- The course accepts project papers that explain motivation, related work, system/prototype design, experiment design, deliverables, and limitations.
- The examples are closer to proposals than finished empirical CHI papers.
- They use plain language and direct descriptions rather than dense academic writing.
- They include explicit independent/dependent variables and planned measures.

## How AirDesk Can Be Stronger

- Use a cleaner ACM manuscript structure.
- Make the system contribution explicit.
- Include actual prototype artifacts and evidence, not only planned tasks.
- State limitations honestly instead of burying them.
- Provide a final-package README that helps the grader quickly understand repo, videos, paper, and prototype.
