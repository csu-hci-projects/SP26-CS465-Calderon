# Video Plan

## Short Project Video

Target length: 3-5 minutes.

Suggested structure:

1. Problem: moments when keyboard/mouse are inconvenient.
2. AirDesk idea: mid-air gestures as a secondary command layer.
3. Quick system diagram.
4. Live or replay demo:
   - camera tracking,
   - open palm clutch,
   - gesture candidate,
   - dry-run or guarded action log.
5. Evidence:
   - rule recognizer failed on natural motion,
   - DTW was more promising,
   - current limitations.
6. Closing: what the pilot showed and what comes next.
